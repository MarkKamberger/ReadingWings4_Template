Teacher Procedure
Vocabulary Review
  • Ask teams to have teammates make a tent with their hands when they are ready to tell a word the entire team rated with a "+" and a word the entire team rated with a "?."
  • Use Random Reporter to have teams share one word they know and one word they need to study further. Award team celebration points.
  • Read each word on your chart. Ask students to repeat each word. Explain the meaning and how to use the identification strategy(optional after day 2).
  • Assign partners as either speaker or coach to review the vocabulary words. Teach or model this student routine as necessary.
  • Remind students that only the coach should look at the vocabulary chart. Have students begin.
  • Use Random Reporter to review word pronunciation, meanings, and sentences.
  • Allow students to use their lesson charts on day 1 if they do not know a word or its meaning.
  • Review, as necessary, the directions for finding the vocabulary words in the daily reading and for adding words to the Vocabulary Vault.
  • Find a vocabulary word in your reading.
  • Write the word and the page number where you found it in your journal.
  • Show your teacher your journal on test day!
