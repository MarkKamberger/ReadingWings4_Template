    • Teacher Procedure
    Partner Reading and Team Discussion
  • Pass out sticky notes to each student, and have students begin Partner Reading.
  • Reinforce discussions as students read, reread, restate, and use strategies.
  • Teach or model this student routine as necessary.
  • Direct partners who finish reading ahead of their teammates to look over their Team Talk questions, or have them take turns rereading the pages designated for Fluency in Five.
  • Explain, or have team leaders review, if necessary, the student routine for Team Discussion.
  • Prompt and reinforce students during their discussions.
  • Monitor Think-and-Connect discussions, and guide students' thinking with additional questions or comments.
  • Prompt students to share answers to the Write-On question and to revise them for Class Discussion.
  • Ask teams to work on their graphic organizers if they finish ahead of others.
  • Award team celebration points for use of the team cooperation goal and behavior.
