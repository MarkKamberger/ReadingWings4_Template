Lesson Objective: Students will take a preassessment and be introduced to cooperative learning and teamwork.
By the end of this lesson, students will take a pretest to set up math teams, and will do a simple math activity that introduces the routines of Think-Pair-Share and Zero Noise Signal.
Materials: (Set up the classroom so the students are sitting in groups of four or five.) A letter to parents is linked to the homework icon. Make sure this goes home this evening.
During Teamwork, circulate and listen to students' discussions to get a basic understanding of their number sense and comfort level with math.
Note: You must check the pretests before class tomorrow. This information will help you to place your students into teams. Please follow the instructions in the Participant Booklet to set up your teams.
