Team Cooperation Goal
1. What effect does his missing lunch bag have on Ramon?
2. What is Ramon's main problem in the story?
3. What happens before Jack tells Emily and Ramon about the paint?
a. Ramon smells salami on Jack's breath.
b. They see him wiping mustard on a napkin.
c. They see an empty lunch bag on his desk.
d. The ask Jack about a stain on his shirt.
4. You can conclude from the text that Ramon and Emily -
a. think Jack is innocent.
b. believe Jack stole the sandwich.
c. are playing hooky.
d. think math class is the best of all.
Teacher Procedure
Team Cooperation Goal
  • Point out the team cooperation goal and related behavior on the team score sheet.
  • Describe the behavior, and use a looks like/sounds like chart to add details about the goal and behavior.
  • Tell students that you will award team celebration points to teams that practice the team cooperation goal and related behavior.
