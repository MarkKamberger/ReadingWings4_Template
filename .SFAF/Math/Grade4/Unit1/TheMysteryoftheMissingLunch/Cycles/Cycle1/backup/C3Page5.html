Teacher Procedure
Success Review
  • Hand out team score sheets and team certificates.
  • Point to the Team Celebration Points poster, and celebrate super teams from the previous lesson.
  • Remind students how to earn team celebration points. Remind them that team celebration points help them to become super teams.
  • Guide teams to set new team improvement goals.
  • Have one student from each team write the team improvement goal on the team score sheet. Note each team's goal on the teacher cycle record form.
  • Award team celebration points throughout each instructional day.
  • Keep track of team celebration points as you award them. Transfer points in the team score sheet tally boxes to the Team Celebration Points poster at the end of each day
  • Use the overlay to show team celebration scores.
